# Laser Vibrometer PCB Design

**Category:** Hardware & PCB, Embedded Control
**Role:** Freelance Developer

## Overview
Engineered a complete PCB topology for a sophisticated Laser Vibrometer system. This device required high-precision timing, accurate laser modulation, and fast data acquisition.

## Technical Details
- Designed a reliable **battery charging and management circuit** (BMS) for portable capabilities.
- Developed a highly precise **laser control circuit**.
- Manufactured communication links for PC interfacing, and built a custom firmware module to implement raw high-speed data logging onto an **SD card**.
- Integrated and interfaced with a specialized **TOF (Time-of-Flight)** optical sensor.
