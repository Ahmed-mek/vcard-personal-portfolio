# Hologram Fan Project - TÜBİTAK Project

**Category:** Embedded Control, Power Electronics
**Role:** R&D Embedded Systems Engineer at Zenopix

## Overview
This project, supported by TÜBİTAK, involves the development of an advanced Hologram Fan capable of displaying high-resolution 3D images and videos in mid-air. The system requires extremely precise motor control and innovative power delivery methods.

## Features

### 1 - BLDC Motor Driver
- Implemented the **FOC (Field-Oriented Control)** algorithm to ensure the stability and steadiness of the motor during rotation.
- Designed a highly compact PCB fitting within aerodynamic constraints.
- Employed custom **PID** algorithms to maintain mathematically consistent speed during operation, which is critical for persistence-of-vision (POV) clarity.

### 2 - Wireless Power Transmission
- Understood and applied the principles of power transfer using magnetic resonance.
- Successfully achieved wireless power transmission of **500W** across the rotating gap.
- Fully designed the hardware, programmed the firmware, and controlled the entire energy transfer system.
