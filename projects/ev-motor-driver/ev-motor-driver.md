# Electric Vehicle Motor Driver

**Category:** Hardware & PCB, Embedded Control
**Role:** Project Manager & Hardware Architect
**Event:** International Efficiency Challenge Electric Vehicle Races (2024), Electromobile Category

## Overview
This highly competitive engineering project aimed to develop a totally custom electric drivetrain and power architecture from scratch for a full-sized electric vehicle traversing competition circuits. 

## Key Deliverables
- **Motor Control & Power:** Formally designed a robust, actively cooled motor driver for a high-power **4.5 kW, 72V BLDC** traction motor.
- **Inductive Load Testing:** Designed and assembled a **3 kW** power supply structure (utilizing a 220-72 VAC transformer setup) to simulate induction loads specifically for competition inspection tests.
- **Battery Management (BMS):** Researched and significantly contributed to the development of a complex custom BMS layout for vehicular lithium cells.
- **Charging Architecture:** Aided heavily in the design of the vehicle's onboard logic for SMPS local charging units.

## Leadership
Served as Project Manager. Tasked with ensuring timely part delivery, coordinating component integration algorithms across mechanical and electrical teams, and leading the team to a technically verified finish.
