About me

I am Mechatronics Engineer with a passion for aviation electronics and drone technology, aiming to innovate and manufacture advanced drones. I excel at leading teams and working under pressure, with a strong passion for my work that drives me to continually learn and innovate.

My technical expertise includes working with BLDC motors and drivers, ESCs, PCB design using KiCad and Altium Designer, analog systems, ICs, and wireless power transfer. I'm proficient in C, C#, Python, and MicroPython, with experience in STM32, RP2040, Arduino, embedded Linux, ROS, and RTOS.

What i'm doing

  • Embedded Systems Firmware

    Developing reliable firmware and control algorithms for bare-metal systems using C, C++, and Python. Specializing in RTOS, motor control, and hardware-firmware integration.

  • Hardware & PCB Design

    Designing complex multi-layer PCBs using KiCad and Altium Designer. Integrating motor drivers, microcontrollers, and precision sensors for industrial applications.

  • Power Electronics

    Designing efficient power systems including wireless power transfer up to 500W, Battery Management Systems (BMS), Electric Vehicle components, and SMPS.

  • Control Systems & Robotics

    Designing advanced control algorithms such as PID and FOC. Experience in dynamic system modeling, ROS (Robot Operating System), and embedded data filtering.
